About The Game

Welcome to the world of Monster Survivors, a thrilling roguelike action game where you must stand against overwhelming waves of monstrous creatures. Your mission is simple: survive. With each enemy you defeat, you'll gather vital resources to unlock powerful new abilities and upgrade your character. Each run is a new challenge, a new opportunity to perfect your strategy and push further than before.

How to Play

Use your mouse or keyboard to navigate the treacherous landscapes. Your character will attack the nearest enemies automatically. Your primary goal is to dodge their assaults while collecting the experience gems they drop upon defeat. Leveling up allows you to choose from a selection of random skills to enhance your power for the current session. Choose wisely, as your unique build will determine your fate!

Key Features

  • Endless Hordes: Face off against an ever-growing army of diverse monsters with unique attack patterns.
  • Roguelike Progression: Every run is unique. Adapt your strategy on the fly with hundreds of randomized skill combinations.
  • Character Upgrades: Permanently enhance your survivors' stats and unlock new starting abilities between runs.
  • Epic Boss Fights: Test your skills against colossal bosses that will push your strategic thinking and reflexes to the limit.
  • Play Instantly: No download required. Jump right into the action from your browser on any device.
